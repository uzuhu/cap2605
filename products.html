① Snap-In Terminal Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

Snap-in terminal capacitors feature a secure press-fit mounting design that eliminates the need for screws or additional hardware. This makes them ideal for high-density PCB layouts and compact inverter designs. Our snap-in series covers diameters from 22mm to 90mm, with voltage ratings up to 500V.

② Screw Terminal Aluminum Electrolytic Capacitors

Screw terminal capacitors provide a robust, vibration-resistant connection ideal for high-power industrial applications. Our screw terminal series spans diameters from 30mm to 115mm, making them the preferred choice for UPS systems, welding machines, motor drives, and mining explosion-proof equipment.
