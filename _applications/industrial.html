Capacitors for Industrial Automation

Power filtering and energy storage for PLCs, servo drives, and motor controls

Application Overview

Industrial automation equipment relies on stable DC power for reliable operation. Aluminum electrolytic capacitors provide bulk energy storage, ripple filtering, and voltage stabilization across a wide range of automation devices.

Snap-in types are widely used in compact variable frequency drives and servo amplifiers, while screw terminal types serve in larger power supplies and centralized DC bus systems.
