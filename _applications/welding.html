Capacitors for Welding Equipment

High ripple current capacitors for resistance welding and arc welding machines

Application Overview

Welding equipment demands capacitors capable of handling extremely high ripple currents and repeated charge/discharge cycles. Resistance welding machines use capacitors as energy storage elements that discharge rapidly through the welding tips.

Key requirements include low ESR to minimize heat generation, high ripple current ratings, and mechanical robustness to withstand vibration and thermal cycling.
