Capacitors for UPS & Power Supply

Bulk capacitance and ride-through energy storage for uninterruptible power supplies

Application Overview

In UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems, aluminum electrolytic capacitors provide bulk energy storage on the DC bus, maintaining voltage stability during AC line failures and bridging the transfer time to battery power.

These applications demand capacitors with high capacitance values, low ESR for high ripple current handling, and long service life under continuous operation at 85°C or higher.
