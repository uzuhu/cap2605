Capacitors for Inverter & VFD

DC-link, snubber, and filtering capacitors for variable frequency drives and power inverters

Application Overview

In variable frequency drives (VFDs) and power inverters, aluminum electrolytic capacitors serve as DC-link energy storage and ripple current filtering components. They smooth the DC bus voltage after rectification and provide energy buffering during load transients.

Key requirements for inverter capacitors include high ripple current capability, low ESR, long operational life at elevated temperatures, and stable capacitance over the full temperature range.
