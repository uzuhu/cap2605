About Nantong Huayu Electronics

Founded in 1987 · Specializing in custom aluminum electrolytic capacitors

Company Profile

Nantong Huayu Electronics Co., Ltd. (formerly Nantong Huayu Capacitor Factory) was founded in 1987. We specialize in the R&D, manufacturing, and customization of medium and large specialty aluminum electrolytic capacitors.

Our core product families — snap-in type and screw terminal type aluminum electrolytic capacitors — serve a wide range of industrial applications including inverters, UPS systems, welding equipment, and industrial automation.

Our Positioning

  • Small but precise — focused product range, deep expertise
  • Specialized and strong — custom specifications are our strength
  • 17 product series covering diverse industrial needs
  • OEM/ODM services for custom electrical requirements
